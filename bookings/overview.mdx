# Bookings

Bookings are commercial contracts.

A booking is created explicitly from an accepted quote.

Quote acceptance does not automatically create a booking.

## Booking conversion

Booking conversion copies accepted quote, version, scenario, and line snapshots.

This preserves the commercial boundary between quote and booking.

## Booking passengers

Booking passengers are commercial traveller snapshots.

They do not need to link to master passengers, but they may do so when available.

## Booking rooming

Booking rooming is booking-scoped.

Rooming does not create tour passengers and does not mutate master passenger profiles.
