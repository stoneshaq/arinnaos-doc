# Booking Passengers and Rooming

Booking passengers are commercial traveller snapshots created at booking time.

They may link to master passengers, but that link is optional.

## Snapshot rule

Booking passenger data does not automatically change if the master passenger profile changes later.

## Booking rooms

Booking rooms are scoped to the booking.

Rooming does not create tour passengers and does not mutate master passenger data.
