# Snapshot Strategy

ArinnaOS relies on snapshots to preserve historical truth.

## Quote snapshots

Quote lines store cost, FX, markup, tax, commission, margin, supplier, and pricing snapshots.

## Booking snapshots

Bookings copy accepted quote totals, scenario lineage, booking lines, and passenger assumptions.

## Invoice snapshots

Invoices copy booking totals and booking line snapshots.

## Why snapshots matter

Live supplier rates, FX rates, passenger data, and quote metadata may change later.

Historical commercial records must remain stable.
