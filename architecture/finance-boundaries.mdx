# Finance Boundaries

ArinnaOS has two finance domains.

## Travel finance

Travel finance bills customers for travel products and services.

It includes:

- Bookings
- Customer invoices
- Customer payments
- Payment allocations
- Customer balances
- Financial reconciliation

## SaaS finance

SaaS finance bills organisations for using ArinnaOS.

It includes:

- Organisation subscriptions
- SaaS invoices
- SaaS payments
- SaaS payment allocations
- Subscription lifecycle

## Separation rule

Travel invoices and payments never mix with SaaS invoices and payments.
