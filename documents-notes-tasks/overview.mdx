# Documents, Notes, and Tasks

Documents, notes, and tasks are shared collaboration surfaces across ArinnaOS.

## Documents

Documents can attach to major business records such as customers, suppliers, passengers, quotes, bookings, invoices, payments, and platform records where supported.

## Notes

Notes store internal context and collaboration history.

## Tasks

Tasks help teams track follow-ups, operational work, and internal reminders.

## Activity

Key changes are recorded in activity logs.
