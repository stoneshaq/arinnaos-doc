# Travel Finance

Travel finance in ArinnaOS manages customer-facing invoices and payments.

It is separate from SaaS billing.

## Invoices

Invoices can be created explicitly from bookings.

Invoice lines copy frozen booking line snapshots.

Invoice totals and snapshots remain stable after creation.

## Payments

Payments are receipt records.

Payments can be allocated to invoices.

One payment can allocate across multiple invoices, and one invoice can receive multiple payments.

## Reconciliation

Finance reconciliation updates invoice balance state and customer balances without mutating original invoice totals or snapshots.

## Customer account balances

Customer account balances provide derived finance summaries, including invoiced totals, paid totals, outstanding totals, and credit balances.
