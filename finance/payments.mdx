# Payments

Payments are separate receipt records.

A payment can be allocated to one or more invoices.

An invoice can receive multiple payments.

## Allocation rules

Allocations cannot exceed:

- Remaining unallocated payment amount
- Current invoice balance due

Only completed payments can be allocated.

Cross-currency allocation is intentionally rejected.
