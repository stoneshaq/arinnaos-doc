# Invoices

Invoices are customer-facing finance records.

Invoices are created explicitly and may be generated from bookings.

## Frozen snapshots

Invoice lines copy frozen booking line snapshots.

Invoice totals and snapshots should not be mutated by later quote, booking, supplier, or FX changes.

## Statuses

Invoice statuses include:

- Draft
- Issued
- Partially paid
- Paid
- Cancelled
- Archived
