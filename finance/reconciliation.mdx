# Reconciliation

Finance reconciliation recalculates invoice balances, payment state, and customer account balances.

## Customer account balances

Customer balances can track:

- Total invoiced
- Total paid
- Total outstanding
- Credit balance

## Financial audit events

Finance-specific audit events are recorded for reconciliation, overpayments, credit creation, and balance recalculation.

## Important boundary

Reconciliation updates balance state only. It does not mutate invoice totals or original snapshots.
