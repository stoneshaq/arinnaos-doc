# Supplier Network

Suppliers are the canonical parent aggregate for supplier-facing entities.

A supplier can be a hotel, transport provider, guide, restaurant, activity supplier, or mixed supplier.

## Supplier-owned profiles

Supplier-owned profiles include:

- Supplier contacts
- Hotel profiles
- Hotel rates
- Transport profiles
- Transport rates
- Guide profiles
- Restaurant profiles

## Why supplier ownership matters

This design prevents disconnected hotel, guide, transport, and restaurant records.

It keeps supplier data easier to audit and extend.
