# Supplier Network

Suppliers are the canonical parent records for supplier-facing entities in ArinnaOS.

A supplier can represent a hotel, transport provider, guide, restaurant, activity provider, or mixed supplier.

## Supplier subtypes

Supplier profiles extend the supplier parent record.

Supported supplier layers include:

- Supplier contacts
- Hotel profiles
- Hotel rates
- Transport profiles
- Transport rates
- Guide profiles
- Restaurant profiles

## Why suppliers are parent records

Keeping suppliers as the parent aggregate avoids disconnected hotel, guide, restaurant, or transport records.

This makes supplier relationships cleaner and easier to audit.
