# Hotel Suppliers

Hotel suppliers use a hotel profile attached to a supplier parent record.

## Hotel profile

Hotel profiles store hotel-specific information such as property type, star rating, check-in and check-out time, amenities, location, and operational notes.

## Hotel rates

Hotel rates are stored separately from hotel profiles.

ArinnaOS supports room-based pricing fields:

- Single rate
- Double rate
- Triple rate
- Quad rate
- Extra bed rate

Rates are dated and currency-aware.
