# Plans and Entitlements

SaaS plans define available ArinnaOS product tiers.

## Plan prices

Plans can have monthly and annual prices.

## Entitlements

Entitlements define whether a feature is available.

Examples:

- CRM
- Suppliers
- Tours
- Quotes
- Bookings
- Finance
- Passenger portal
- Marketplace
- API access

## Overrides

Subscription-level entitlement overrides can be used for beta access, sales concessions, or support remediation.
