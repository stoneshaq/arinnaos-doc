# SaaS Billing

SaaS billing manages ArinnaOS subscriptions for tenant organisations.

It is separate from travel finance.

## Plans

Plans define available product tiers.

Plan prices define monthly or annual pricing.

## Entitlements

Entitlements determine which features an organisation can use.

## Usage limits

Usage limits determine whether an organisation is within plan allowance.

Hard limits can block actions. Soft limits can warn while allowing temporary overflow.

## SaaS invoices and payments

SaaS invoices and payments use dedicated tables and workflows.

They do not mix with customer travel invoices or payments.

## Lifecycle

Subscription lifecycle supports trial, active, past due, suspended, cancelled, and expired states.
