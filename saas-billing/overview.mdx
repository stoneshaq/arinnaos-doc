# SaaS Billing

SaaS billing manages ArinnaOS subscriptions for organisations.

It is separate from travel finance.

## SaaS billing includes

- SaaS plans
- Plan prices
- Entitlements
- Usage limits
- Organisation subscriptions
- Usage counters
- SaaS invoices
- SaaS payments
- Lifecycle automation
