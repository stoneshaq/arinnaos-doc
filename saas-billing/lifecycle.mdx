# Subscription Lifecycle

Organisation subscriptions can move through lifecycle states.

## Supported states

- Trial
- Active
- Past due
- Suspended
- Cancelled
- Expired

## Renewal

Renewal processing can generate SaaS invoices and update subscription period state.

## Downgrades

Downgrades are scheduled and applied at renewal boundaries.

Tenant data is never deleted because of plan changes.
