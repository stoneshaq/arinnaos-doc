# Passengers

Passengers are master traveller profiles.

Passenger records are reusable across future quotes, bookings, and departures.

## Passenger profile data

Passenger records can store:

- Name
- Contact details
- Nationality
- Passport information
- Insurance information
- Dietary notes
- Allergy notes
- Medical notes
- Mobility notes
- Emergency contact details

## Documents

Passport, visa, and insurance files should be attached through the document system.
