# Passengers

Passengers are master traveller profiles.

Passenger profiles are reusable across bookings, quotes, and departures.

## Master passenger profile

Passenger records can include:

- Name
- Contact details
- Nationality
- Passport information
- Insurance details
- Dietary notes
- Allergy notes
- Medical notes
- Mobility notes
- Emergency contact

## Passenger visas

Visa records live separately from the passenger master profile.

## Tour passengers

Tour-specific operational information belongs to tour passenger records, including:

- Room type
- Room number
- Seat number
- Roommate
- Arrival flight
- Departure flight
- Tour notes
- Special requirements
