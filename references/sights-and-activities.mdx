# Sights and Activities

Sights and activities are organisation-scoped reference data.

## Sights

Sights represent attractions or points of interest.

A sight may belong to a place.

## Activities

Activities represent reusable experiences or service items.

Activities may link to:

- Supplier
- Place
- Sight

If links are present, they must belong to the same organisation.
