# Audit Logs

ArinnaOS records important events through activity logging and finance-specific audit events.

## Activity logs

Activity logs track user and system actions across modules.

## Financial audit events

Financial audit events provide additional traceability for reconciliation, overpayments, credits, and account balances.

## Why audit history matters

Audit trails help teams understand what changed, who changed it, and when it happened.
