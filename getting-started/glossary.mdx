# Glossary

## Organisation

A tenant within ArinnaOS.

## Membership

A relationship between a user and an organisation.

## Customer

A buyer or client.

## Supplier

A provider of travel services.

## Passenger

A traveller.

## Quote

A commercial proposal.

## Booking

A confirmed commercial arrangement.

## Invoice

A financial document requesting payment.

## Payment

A recorded customer payment.
