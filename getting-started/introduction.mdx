# Welcome to ArinnaOS

ArinnaOS is a modern operating platform for travel businesses.

It combines CRM, supplier management, tours, passengers, quotes, bookings, finance, SaaS billing, and platform administration into a single system.

## Who is ArinnaOS for?

ArinnaOS is designed for:

- Destination Management Companies (DMCs)
- Tour Operators
- Travel Agencies
- Travel Advisors
- Group Travel Specialists

## What makes ArinnaOS different?

ArinnaOS is built around operational workflows instead of disconnected modules.

A customer can move through:

Lead → Customer → Quote → Booking → Invoice → Payment

while preserving a complete history.
