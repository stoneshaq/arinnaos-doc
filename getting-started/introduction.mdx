# Introduction

ArinnaOS is a modern operating platform for travel businesses.

It brings together CRM, supplier management, tours, passengers, quotes, bookings, finance, SaaS billing, and platform administration into one connected system.

## Who is ArinnaOS for?

ArinnaOS is designed for:

- Destination Management Companies
- Tour operators
- Travel agencies
- Travel advisors
- Group travel specialists
- Incoming operators

## Product philosophy

ArinnaOS is designed around operational continuity.

A customer can move through:

Lead → Customer → Quote → Booking → Invoice → Payment

while preserving history, commercial snapshots, documents, notes, tasks, and activity records.
