# Core Concepts

ArinnaOS is organised around a few core concepts.

## Organisation

An organisation is a tenant in ArinnaOS. Each travel company, DMC, agency, or operator has its own organisation workspace.

Data is scoped to the organisation.

## User

Users access ArinnaOS through invitations. Users can belong to one or more organisations depending on permissions and membership.

## Roles and permissions

Access is permission-based. ArinnaOS uses roles to group permissions, but permissions decide what a user can view or manage.

## CRM

CRM contains advisors, customers, and leads. These records help manage relationships before and after travel is booked.

## Supplier network

Suppliers are the parent records for hotels, transport providers, guides, restaurants, and other travel service providers.

## Tour templates

Tour templates are reusable products. They define the structure of a tour, but they do not represent operating dates.

## Tour departures

A departure is a scheduled operating instance of a tour template version.

## Passengers

Passengers are master traveller profiles. Tour-specific state such as room, seat, flight, and roommate information belongs to departure participation records.

## Quotes

Quotes are commercial proposals. Quote versions and scenarios preserve pricing snapshots.

## Bookings

Bookings are commercial contracts created explicitly from accepted quotes.

## Finance

Travel finance manages customer invoices, payments, allocations, balances, and reconciliation.

## SaaS billing

SaaS billing manages ArinnaOS subscriptions, plans, entitlements, usage limits, SaaS invoices, and SaaS payments. It is separate from travel finance.
