# Core Concepts

ArinnaOS is built around clear domain boundaries.

## Organisation

An organisation is a tenant workspace. Data belongs to an organisation and is isolated from other organisations.

## User

Users access ArinnaOS by invitation. A user may belong to one or more organisations if they are invited and assigned membership.

## Roles and permissions

ArinnaOS uses role-backed permissions. Roles group permissions, but permissions decide access.

There is no simple `users.role` column.

## CRM

CRM stores advisor profiles, customers, and leads.

## Supplier network

Suppliers are the canonical parent records for hotels, transport providers, guides, restaurants, and other supplier-facing profiles.

## Tour templates

Tour templates are reusable products. They define itinerary structure but not operating dates.

## Tour departures

Departures are scheduled operating instances of specific tour template versions.

## Passengers

Passengers are master traveller profiles. Departure-specific details live in tour passenger records.

## Quotes

Quotes are commercial proposals. Quote versions and scenarios preserve pricing snapshots.

## Bookings

Bookings are commercial contracts created explicitly from accepted quotes.

## Travel finance

Travel finance manages customer invoices, payments, allocations, reconciliation, and balances.

## SaaS billing

SaaS billing manages ArinnaOS subscriptions for tenant organisations. It is separate from travel finance.
