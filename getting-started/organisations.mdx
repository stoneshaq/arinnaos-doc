# Organisations

An organisation represents a tenant workspace in ArinnaOS.

Each travel company has its own organisation scope.

## Organisation ownership

Most operational records belong to an organisation, including:

- Customers
- Leads
- Suppliers
- Tours
- Departures
- Passengers
- Quotes
- Bookings
- Invoices
- Payments

## Isolation

Organisation data is isolated by design.

Cross-organisation access is denied unless a platform-level admin action explicitly allows it.

## Suspension and archive

Platform admins can suspend or archive organisations.

Suspension and archive do not delete tenant travel data.
