# Organisations

Organisations are the tenant boundary in ArinnaOS.

Most business data belongs to one organisation.

## Organisation-scoped records

Examples include:

- Customers
- Leads
- Suppliers
- Tours
- Departures
- Passengers
- Quotes
- Bookings
- Invoices
- Payments

## Suspension and archive

Suspension blocks access without deleting data.

Archive is recoverable and soft-delete based.
