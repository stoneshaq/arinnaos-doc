# Organisations

Organisations are the primary boundary in ArinnaOS.

## Data Ownership

All records belong to an organisation.

Examples:

- Customers
- Suppliers
- Tours
- Quotes
- Bookings
- Finance records

## Isolation

Users cannot access records belonging to another organisation unless explicitly authorised.

## Organisation Lifecycle

Organisations may be:

- Trial
- Active
- Suspended
- Archived
