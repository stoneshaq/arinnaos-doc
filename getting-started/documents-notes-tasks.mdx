# Documents, Notes and Tasks

Documents, notes, and tasks appear throughout ArinnaOS.

## Documents

Documents may be attached to:

- Customers
- Suppliers
- Passengers
- Bookings

## Notes

Notes provide historical context.

Notes should be used for information that may help future users.

## Tasks

Tasks help teams track work and follow-up actions.
