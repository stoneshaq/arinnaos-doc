# Tour Departures

Tour departures are operating instances of specific tour template versions.

## Template version binding

Departures link to a specific tour template version.

A template snapshot is stored at departure creation and remains stable.

## Departure management

Departures can include:

- Departure number
- Date range
- Status
- Capacity
- Definite flag
- Guide assignments
- Transport assignments
- Accommodation records
- Service confirmations

## Operational rule

Departure records are operational. They do not directly create passengers, bookings, invoices, or payments.
