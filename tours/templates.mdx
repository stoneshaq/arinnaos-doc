# Tour Templates

Tour templates are reusable travel products.

They do not contain operating dates.

## Versioning

Tour templates support version lineage.

A new version can be created from a published template. The published source remains unchanged, and the new version becomes a draft.

## Days

Template days support:

- Day number
- Title
- Day type
- Breakfast, lunch, dinner flags
- Start, end, and overnight places

## Day items

Day items can represent activities, transfers, meals, accommodation, sights, free time, flights, ferries, trains, notes, or custom items.

## Accommodation planning

Structured accommodation planning is stored separately from day items.
