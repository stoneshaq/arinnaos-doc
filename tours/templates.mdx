# Tour Templates

Tour templates are reusable travel products.

They describe the structure of a tour but do not represent operating dates.

## Versioning

Tour templates support versioning.

A template version can be published, and future departures can link to a specific version.

This protects operating history because departures reference a specific template version, not a mutable product record.

## Tour template days

Tour template days may include:

- Day title
- Day type
- Meal flags
- Start, end, and overnight places
- Day items
- Accommodation planning

## Day items

Day items can represent:

- Activity
- Transfer
- Meal
- Accommodation
- Sight
- Free time
- Flight
- Ferry
- Train
- Note
- Custom item
