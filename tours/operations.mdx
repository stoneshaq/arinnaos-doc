# Departure Operations

Departure operations manage the practical delivery of scheduled tours.

## Guide assignments

Guide assignments enforce organisation consistency and date-range checks.

Only one primary guide is allowed per departure.

## Transport assignments

Transport assignments enforce transport profile and rate consistency.

## Accommodation records

Accommodation records connect departure days to planned hotel arrangements.

## Service confirmations

Departure services track confirmation state for operational services such as suppliers, activities, or arrangements.
