# Leads

Leads represent enquiries or opportunities before they become customers or formal quotes.

## Lead source

Lead source is definition-driven.

Common default sources include:

- Website
- Email
- Phone
- Referral
- Manual

## Assignment

Leads can be assigned to active users in the same organisation.

## Conversion

A lead can be converted into a customer while preserving lineage.
