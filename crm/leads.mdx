# Leads

Leads represent enquiries or sales opportunities before they become customers or quotes.

## Lead workflow

A lead can be created from channels such as website, email, phone, referral, or manual entry.

Lead source is definition-driven, so organisations can customise source categories later.

## Assignment

Leads can be assigned to active users in the same organisation.

Suspended, left, or cross-organisation users cannot be assigned.

## Conversion

A lead can be converted into a customer.

Conversion preserves the lead lineage so teams can understand where the customer came from.
