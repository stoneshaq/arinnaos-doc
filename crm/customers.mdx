# Customers

Customers represent buyers, clients, companies, or travellers connected to a sales opportunity.

Customer records sit inside the CRM layer and can be linked to quotes, bookings, invoices, payments, passengers, advisors, notes, documents, and tasks.

## What customers store

Customer profiles may include:

- Name or company name
- Email and phone details
- Customer status
- Customer type
- Advisor relationship
- Lead conversion lineage
- Notes and internal context

## Customer relationships

Customers can be connected to:

- Advisors
- Leads
- Passengers
- Quotes
- Bookings
- Invoices
- Payments

## Activity history

Important customer actions are recorded through ArinnaOS activity logging so teams can understand what happened and when.
