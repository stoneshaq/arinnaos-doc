# Customers

Customers represent buyers, clients, companies, or traveller contacts.

A customer can be connected to leads, advisors, passengers, quotes, bookings, invoices, and payments.

## Customer data

Customer records may include:

- Customer number
- Name or company name
- Customer type
- Customer status
- Email and phone details
- Advisor linkage
- Lead conversion lineage
- Notes

## Customer lifecycle

Customers may be active, inactive, or archived depending on organisation workflow.

## Documents, notes, and tasks

Customer records support documents, notes, and tasks.
