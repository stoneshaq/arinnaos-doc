# Scenarios and Lines

Quote scenarios allow alternatives inside a version.

Examples:

- Good / better / best
- 4-star vs 5-star hotels
- Different departures
- With or without optional services

## Quote lines

Quote lines store:

- Source cost
- FX rate
- Converted cost
- Markup
- Discount
- Commission
- Tax
- Sell price
- Margin
- Supplier snapshot
- Pricing snapshot

Old quote lines do not re-read live supplier rates.
