# Quotes

Quotes are commercial proposals for customer opportunities.

ArinnaOS separates quote requests, quotes, versions, scenarios, and lines.

## Quote request

A quote request captures the customer enquiry before pricing.

## Quote

A quote is the mutable workflow head.

## Quote version

A quote version is an immutable priced snapshot.

## Quote scenario

A scenario is an alternative option inside a quote version.

## Quote line

A quote line stores frozen pricing inputs and outputs.
