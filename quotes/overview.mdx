# Quotes

Quotes are commercial containers for customer opportunities.

ArinnaOS separates quote requests, quotes, quote versions, scenarios, and lines.

## Quote request

A quote request captures the enquiry or brief before formal pricing.

## Quote

The quote is the mutable workflow head.

It tracks the customer, advisor, status, sent state, acceptance state, and commercial messaging.

## Quote version

A quote version is an immutable priced snapshot.

When pricing changes, a new version should be created instead of mutating the old one.

## Quote scenario

Scenarios allow alternatives inside a version, such as good, better, best options.

## Quote lines

Quote lines store frozen pricing data, including source cost, FX, markup, discount, commission, tax, sell price, and margin.
