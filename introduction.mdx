# ArinnaOS

ArinnaOS is a multi-tenant operating platform for DMCs, tour operators, travel agencies, and travel advisors.

It brings CRM, suppliers, tours, departures, passengers, quotes, bookings, finance, SaaS billing, documents, notes, tasks, notifications, and platform administration into one connected workspace.

## Who ArinnaOS is for

ArinnaOS is designed for travel businesses that manage complex operations:

- Destination management companies
- Tour operators
- Group travel companies
- Travel agencies
- Travel advisors
- Incoming operators
- Specialist travel teams

## What ArinnaOS helps you control

- Customers, leads, and advisors
- Suppliers, rates, guides, hotels, transport, and restaurants
- Tour products and scheduled departures
- Passenger information and operational participation
- Quotes, pricing snapshots, bookings, and rooming
- Invoices, payments, reconciliation, and account balances
- SaaS subscriptions, entitlements, usage limits, and platform administration

## Invite-only by design

ArinnaOS is an invite-only B2B SaaS platform.

There is no public registration or self-service organisation signup. Platform admins create organisations, and organisation admins invite users into their workspace.
