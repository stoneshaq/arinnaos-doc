# ArinnaOS

ArinnaOS is a multi-tenant operating platform for DMCs, tour operators, travel agencies, and travel advisors.

It brings together CRM, suppliers, tour operations, passengers, quotes, bookings, finance, SaaS billing, documents, tasks, notes, notifications, and platform administration into one connected system.

## What ArinnaOS helps you manage

- Customer relationships
- Leads and enquiries
- Supplier networks
- Tour templates and departures
- Passenger profiles
- Quotes and booking conversion
- Invoices, payments, and reconciliation
- Documents, notes, tasks, and activity history
- Organisation users, roles, and permissions

## Invite-only by design

ArinnaOS is designed as an invite-only B2B SaaS platform.

There is no public self-registration. Platform admins create organisations, and organisation admins invite their users.

## Product philosophy

ArinnaOS is built around three ideas:

- Keep each organisation isolated and secure
- Preserve commercial and financial snapshots
- Make complex travel operations easier to control
