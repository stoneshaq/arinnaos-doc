# Platform Administration

Platform administration is for ArinnaOS operators.

Platform admins manage organisations, subscriptions, platform settings, support summaries, and entitlement overrides.

## Platform admin capabilities

Platform admins can:

- Create organisations
- Update organisations
- Suspend organisations
- Reactivate organisations
- Archive organisations
- Assign SaaS subscriptions
- Change plans
- Apply entitlement overrides
- View support summaries

## Support role

Support users can access read-only support summaries where permitted.

## Recoverable operations

Suspension and archiving do not delete tenant travel data.
