# Platform Overview

Platform administration tools help ArinnaOS operators manage organisations, subscriptions, settings, and support.

## What this page covers

This page introduces the main concepts, boundaries, and workflows for platform overview.

## Key ideas

- Records are organisation-scoped where applicable.
- Permissions control access.
- Activity history records important changes.
- Documents, notes, and tasks are available where supported.
- Commercial and financial snapshots remain stable where required.
