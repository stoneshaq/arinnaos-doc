# Platform Administration

Platform administration is used by ArinnaOS operators.

## Platform admins can

- Create organisations
- Update organisations
- Suspend organisations
- Reactivate organisations
- Archive organisations
- Assign subscriptions
- Change plans
- Apply entitlement overrides
- Manage platform settings
- View support summaries

## Safe operations

Suspension and archive do not delete tenant travel data.
